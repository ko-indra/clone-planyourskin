All At Once Water Cream

Rp259.000 Original price was: Rp259.000.Rp108.780Current price is: Rp108.780.

Hydration that feels like nothing, but does everything. Our All At Once Water Cream is the ultimate “glass of water” for your skin. Its unique water-break technology transforms the cream into a refreshing burst of moisture the moment it touches your skin, absorbing instantly with zero sticky residue.

It’s more than just a moisturizer; it’s a skincare-makeup hybrid. With 5% Niacinamide to control excess sebum and brighten dark spots, plus a Peptide Complex to keep skin firm, it creates a smooth, matte-yet-hydrated canvas that grips makeup perfectly all day long.

A super-lightweight moisturizer that bursts into water upon contact, delivering instant hydration without the grease. Formulated with 5% Niacinamide and Peptide Complex to brighten skin, control oil, and strengthen the barrier. The perfect non-sticky base for makeup or daily wear for oily & combination skin.

Available in 30ml

BENEFITS
  • Water-Break Texture: Transforms into refreshing water droplets for instant, weightless absorption.
  • Makeup-Gripping Primer: Creates a smooth, non-greasy canvas that helps makeup last longer.
  • Oil Control & Brightening: 5% Niacinamide helps control excess oil and brighten the appearance of dark spots.
  • Skin Barrier Support: Fortified with Ceramide NP to lock in moisture and protect against environmental stressors.
  • Soothing: Contains Allantoin and Adenosine to calm skin and reduce signs of aging.

HOW TO USE
Apply a pump or two evenly onto the face and neck as the last step of your AM skincare routine (before sunscreen). Pro-Tip: Use it as a hydrating primer before applying foundation or cushion for a flawless, long-lasting finish that won’t pill or slide.
INGREDIENTS
Water, Cyclopentasiloxane, Niacinamide, Glycerin, Cyclohexasi-loxane, Butylene Glycol, Dimethicone, 1,2-Hexanediol, Sodium Chloride, Cetyl PEG/PPG-10/1 Dimethicone, Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride, Phenoxyethanol, Lauryl PEG-9 Polydimethylsiloxyethyl Dimethicone, Dimethicone/PEG-10/15 Crosspolymer, Silica, Sodium Citrate, Hydrogenated Lecithin, Sodium Hyaluronate, Adenosine, Panthenol, Allantoin, Dimethicone Crosspolymer, Carbomer,Ceramide NP, Dipotassium Glycyrrhizate, Polysorbate 20, Sodium Phytate, Dipropylene Glycol, Palmitoyl Tripeptide-1, Tocopherol, Palmitoyl Tetrapeptide-7, Pentylene Glycol, Choleth-24.
LEARN MORE
Why 5% Niacinamide? We use an optimal 5% concentration because it’s the “sweet spot” for results without irritation. At this level, Niacinamide effectively regulates oil production, minimizes the appearance of pores, and brightens uneven skin tone acne care, making it the perfect daily defender for a fresh, shine-free glow.
